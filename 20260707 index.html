Series premise

Most leadership content teaches tactics. This series teaches frameworks — the mental models that determine whether tactics compound. Each episode draws on a proven leadership development tradition and refracts it through the IFS Commercial Maturity Model, asking a single question at every level: does mastering this make a commercial practitioner measurably more effective at capturing and creating value? The series runs personal-to-strategic: from how you manage your own time, to how you lead a team, to how you shape commercial strategy at scale. The parallel Coaching & Empowerment track sits alongside for leaders who are simultaneously managing people.

The new urgency: AI is compounding the reach and impact of every commercial practitioner. Those who have internalized strong leadership and commercial frameworks will use AI to go further, faster. Those who haven't will find that AI accelerates the gap. This is the right time to build the foundation.

Primary intellectual sources

  • IFS Commercial Maturity Model — five-level, five-dimension value capture framework
  • Paul J. Meyer — framework founder; crystallised thought, goal-directed action
  • Patrick Lencioni — Organizational health; the Five Dysfunctions of a Team
  • Willie Pietersen — Strategic Learning model; strategy as a process, not an event
  • Jim Collins — Good to Great; Level 5 leadership; flywheel effect
  • Richard Leider — Purpose and calling in leadership
  • Cal Newport — Deep work; career capital theory
  • Leadership Management International (LMI) — principles from five recommended programmes: Personal Productivity, Personal Leadership, Motivational Leadership, Strategic Leadership, Coaching and Empowerment

How to use this series

Sequential curriculumRun episodes in order as a structured development programme, starting with Group One. Designed to build progressively — personal foundations first, strategic impact last.
Entry-point matchingCommercial team members can enter at their current maturity level. Experienced leaders may start at Group Three or Group Four without losing value.
Parallel trackThe Coaching & Empowerment track (C1–C6) is for leaders with direct reports and can be run alongside any group. Not required for individual contributors.
Synthesis finessingEach episode synthesises the development framework material and applies the commercial maturity lens. Scripts require original "why now" framing specific to IFS and the AI context before recording.
